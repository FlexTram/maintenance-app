# FlexTram Maintenance App — Quick Start Guide

## Getting Started
- Open the app at **maintenance-app-liart.vercel.app**
- Tap **Continue with Google** and sign in with your work account
- You'll land on the **Home Dashboard** showing fleet status and recent activity

## Logging an Inspection or Repair
- Tap **Scan QR Code or Enter Serial # Manually**
- Scan a tram's QR code or type the tram number, serial number, or QR ID
- Select the tram from search results
- Tap **+ Log Inspection** or **+ Log Repair**
- Fill out the form, add photos if needed, and submit

## Logging a Drop-Off
- From the home screen, tap **Log Drop-Off**
- Enter event info, select trams, check condition on each, and sign off

## Changing Tram Status
- Open any tram's profile page
- Tap **Change Status**
- Select In Service, Out of Service, or Pending
- Add a note explaining why, then save

## Voiding a Record
- Find the record on a tram's profile page or the home screen
- Tap **Void Record**
- Enter a reason and tap **Confirm Void**
- The record moves to the Voided Records section
- Voiding a status change automatically reverts the tram to its previous status

## Viewing Records & Fleet Status
- Tap **View all records** to see the full fleet list and all service history
- Use the filter tabs (All, In Service, Out of Service, Pending) to narrow down
- Tap any tram to see its full profile, timeline, and documents

## Reference Documents
- Tap **Quick Document Reference** for technical drawings, wiring diagrams, tow vehicle specs, and operating procedures
- Documents are cached for offline access

## Offline Use
- The app works offline for viewing equipment and service history
- Records created offline will sync automatically when you're back online
- Login and document uploads require an internet connection
